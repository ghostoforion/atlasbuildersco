Warranty Questions

Warranty FAQs

Everything you need to know about making a claim, transferring coverage, and understanding your protection.

How do I file a warranty claim?

Filing a warranty claim is simple. Call our dedicated warranty hotline at (800) 555-1234 or email warranty@atlasbuildersco.com with your project number and a description of the concern. A service coordinator will follow up within 24 hours to schedule an assessment.

Is the warranty transferable?

Yes — our workmanship warranty is fully transferable to subsequent homeowners at no charge. Simply notify us of the ownership change and we will update the warranty documentation. This is a valuable selling point when listing your home.

What voids the warranty?

The warranty may be voided by unauthorized structural modifications, damage caused by natural disasters or acts of God, neglected routine maintenance, or work performed on covered components by uncertified third parties. Your warranty documentation will detail all exclusions in full.

How long until someone responds?

We guarantee a response to all warranty inquiries within 48 hours or less — often the same business day. For urgent structural or safety concerns, we prioritize emergency response and will dispatch a crew as quickly as possible.
