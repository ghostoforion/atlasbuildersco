Getting Started

General Questions

Everything you need to know before reaching out to Atlas Builders Co.

How do I get started?

Simply fill out our free estimate form or call (800) 555-1234. A local project specialist will contact you within 24 hours to schedule your free in-home consultation. There's no obligation and no cost to you.

What areas do you serve?

Atlas Builders Co. operates in 47 states across the continental United States. Enter your ZIP code on our estimate form to confirm we serve your area. We're constantly expanding into new markets, so check back if we're not in your region yet.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Atlas Builders Co. is fully licensed in every state we operate, carries comprehensive general liability insurance, and all our crews are bonded. We're happy to provide proof of licensing and insurance upon request.

How long have you been in business?

Atlas Builders Co. was founded in 1998 and has been serving homeowners for over 25 years. We've completed more than 50,000 projects nationwide and have earned a 4.9-star rating from over 30,000 verified customer reviews.

Do you use subcontractors?

We maintain our own network of factory-trained crews. When we do partner with specialists, they are thoroughly vetted, fully licensed, and held to the same exacting quality standards as our direct employees. You'll never have an unknown crew on your property.

The Work Itself

Construction & Renovation

Common questions about our process, timelines, and how we handle your project from start to finish.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A roofing replacement typically takes 1–3 days. A kitchen remodel generally runs 3–6 weeks. A full new home construction project ranges from 6–12 months. Your project specialist will provide a detailed schedule during your estimate.

Do you handle permits?

Yes. Atlas Builders Co. pulls all required building permits on your behalf. We handle the paperwork, scheduling of inspections, and all communication with local building departments. You don't have to worry about a thing.

What if I'm not happy with the work?

Your satisfaction is guaranteed. If something isn't right, we'll make it right — at no additional cost to you. Our project managers conduct a thorough final walkthrough with you before we consider any job complete, and our warranty coverage ensures you're protected long after we're done.

Can I make changes during construction?

Yes, within reason. We understand that needs and preferences can evolve during a project. Any changes are handled through a formal change order process, which documents the modification, any impact on cost, and any adjustment to the project timeline. Nothing changes without your written approval.

Do I need to be home during the work?

You're not required to be home for routine daily work, though we always appreciate a walkthrough at the start and end of significant phases. Your project manager will keep you informed of progress and will schedule any decision-point check-ins at your convenience.

Money Matters

Financing & Payments

Answers to the most common questions about costs, payment schedules, and financing options.

When do I pay?

Our standard payment structure involves a deposit at project start, progress payments tied to key milestones during construction, and a final payment upon satisfactory completion and your sign-off. The full payment schedule is outlined clearly in your contract before any work begins.

Do you offer financing?

Yes. We offer flexible financing options including $0 down on qualifying projects, low APR plans with terms from 12 to 120 months, and same-day approval. Pre-qualification takes under 5 minutes and won't affect your credit score. Visit our Financing page to learn more.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept personal or cashier's checks, ACH bank transfers, and all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. Financing payments are handled directly through our lending partners.

Will my homeowner's insurance cover any of this?

Possibly — especially for storm, hail, wind, or water damage repairs. We have extensive experience working with insurance companies and can help you navigate the claims process. Our team will document the damage thoroughly and communicate directly with your adjuster to help maximize your claim.
