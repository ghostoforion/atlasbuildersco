Common Questions

Financing FAQs

Here are answers to the questions we hear most often about our financing programs.

What credit score do I need to qualify?

We work with a wide range of credit profiles. While higher scores unlock the most competitive rates, our lending partners offer programs designed for many different credit situations. The best way to find out is to apply — pre-qualification won't affect your score.

How long does the application take?

Under 5 minutes. Our streamlined online application is designed to be quick and straightforward. You'll typically receive a pre-qualification decision in just a few minutes after submitting.

Will applying affect my credit score?

Pre-qualification uses a soft credit pull, which has no impact on your credit score whatsoever. A hard inquiry only occurs if you choose to move forward with a specific loan offer.

Can I pay off my loan early?

Absolutely. There are never any prepayment penalties on any of our financing plans. Pay off your balance at any time without any additional fees or charges.
