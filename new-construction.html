Complete Coverage

What's Included in Every New Construction Build

Atlas Builders Co. handles every phase of your new home construction — no subcontracting surprises, no hidden scopes. Here's what's covered from start to finish.

Foundation & Framing

We pour and inspect every foundation to the strictest local code requirements, then frame your home using premium kiln-dried lumber for structural integrity that lasts generations.

Electrical & Plumbing

All electrical and plumbing work is performed by licensed master tradespeople and inspected at every stage. We spec for the future, including EV-ready panels and whole-home water filtration readiness.

Interior Finishes

From luxury vinyl plank flooring and custom cabinetry to designer tile and premium fixtures, our interior finish packages are curated to provide exceptional quality at every price point.

Landscaping & Exterior

Curb appeal matters. Every Atlas build includes grading, seeding, and a base landscaping package, along with your choice of siding, trim, and exterior door and window combinations.

HVAC Systems

We install high-efficiency HVAC systems sized specifically for your home's square footage and climate zone, ensuring year-round comfort and lower monthly utility bills.

Smart Home Options

Every Atlas home is pre-wired for smart home integration. Choose from our Smart Start package or fully customized systems including automated lighting, security, climate, and entertainment.
