Popular Renovation Projects

From targeted room updates to ambitious whole-home transformations, Atlas Builders Co. has the expertise to handle renovation projects of every size and complexity.

Kitchen Remodels

Reimagine the heart of your home with custom cabinetry, quartz countertops, designer tile backsplashes, and professional-grade appliance packages tailored to how you cook and live.

Bathroom Renovations

From luxurious master bath retreats with freestanding soaker tubs to efficient and beautiful guest bath refreshes, our bathroom renovation teams deliver stunning results every time.

Room Additions

Need more space? We design and build seamless room additions — in-law suites, home offices, sunrooms, master bedroom expansions — that blend perfectly with your existing architecture.

Living Space Updates

Refresh your living room, dining room, or open-plan areas with updated flooring, trim, built-ins, lighting, and paint to create a cohesive, modern feel throughout your main living spaces.

Basement Finishing

Our Renovation Process

We know that living through a renovation can be stressful, which is why we've engineered a process that keeps you informed, protected, and as comfortable as possible from day one. Your dedicated project manager serves as your single point of contact throughout the entire project, handling permits, trades coordination, material scheduling, and quality control so you don't have to.

Before a single wall comes down, we finalize your design selections, secure all required permits, and walk you through a detailed project schedule. During construction, you'll receive regular progress updates and have 24/7 access to your project dashboard. At completion, we conduct a thorough punch-list walkthrough with you to ensure every detail meets the Atlas standard.

  • Dedicated project manager assigned
  • Detailed upfront project schedule
  • All permits pulled and managed for you
  • Regular progress updates
  • Final walkthrough & satisfaction guarantee
