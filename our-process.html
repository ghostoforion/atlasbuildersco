Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to the questions we hear most often from homeowners considering their first project with Atlas.

How long does construction take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and type. A roof replacement may take just 1–2 days, while a full new home construction typically ranges from 4–8 months. During your free estimate, your project specialist will provide a detailed timeline specific to your project.

Do you handle permits?

Yes — we handle all permitting, inspections, and regulatory filings from start to finish. Our team is experienced with local building codes across all 47 states we serve, so you'll never need to step foot in a permit office.

What areas do you serve?

Atlas Builders Co. currently serves homeowners in 47 states nationwide. Enter your ZIP code during the estimate request and we'll confirm local crew availability in your area right away.

What warranties do you offer?

We offer a lifetime workmanship warranty on all labor, manufacturer-backed warranties on materials, and a 5-year service response guarantee. Visit our Warranties page to learn more about what's covered.
