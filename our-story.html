From Humble Beginnings to National Excellence

In 1998, a family in Columbus, Ohio had a simple but powerful belief: that homeowners deserved honest craftsmanship, fair pricing, and a builder they could actually trust. With that belief as their foundation, Atlas Builders Co. was born — not in a boardroom, but on a job site, with calloused hands and a commitment to doing things right.

What started as a single crew serving central Ohio grew steadily through word of mouth, one satisfied homeowner at a time. By the mid-2000s, Atlas had expanded into neighboring states, bringing that same family-instilled work ethic and dedication to quality to thousands of new communities. We never chased growth for its own sake — we grew because our customers asked us to come to them.

Today, Atlas Builders Co. operates in 47 states with over 500 trained crews and more than 50,000 completed homes. But no matter how large we've grown, the values that started us in a garage in Columbus remain at the heart of everything we do. Every project, every crew, every nail — it all comes back to that original promise of honest, excellent work.
