Full-Service Solutions

Our Basement & Foundation Services

From structural repair to full basement finishing, Atlas Builders Co. offers a complete range of below-grade services to protect your home and expand your living space.

Foundation Inspection

Our certified structural inspectors conduct a thorough evaluation of your foundation — inside and out — using industry-leading diagnostic tools to identify cracks, settlement, lateral movement, and moisture intrusion with precision.

Waterproofing Systems

We install both interior and exterior waterproofing systems including drainage tile, sump pump systems, interior drain boards, and wall membrane coatings to keep your basement permanently dry regardless of groundwater conditions.

Foundation Repair

From helical pier underpinning for settling foundations to carbon fiber straps and wall anchors for bowing walls, we use engineered repair systems backed by structural warranties to permanently stabilize your home's foundation.

Basement Finishing

Transform raw, unfinished square footage into a fully functional living area. Our basement finishing teams handle framing, insulation, drywall, flooring, electrical, lighting, and all finish carpentry for a complete, move-in-ready space.

Egress Windows

Egress windows are required by code for any habitable basement bedroom and dramatically improve natural light and ventilation throughout your lower level. We handle all excavation, installation, and permits.

Crawl Space Encapsulation

A properly encapsulated crawl space reduces humidity, eliminates mold risk, improves indoor air quality, and lowers energy bills. Our full-encapsulation systems include vapor barriers, insulation, dehumidification, and drainage solutions.

Warning Signs You Should Never Ignore

Foundation and basement problems rarely appear overnight — they develop gradually, and the earlier they're caught, the less costly and disruptive the repair. If you've noticed any of the following in or around your home, it's time to call Atlas for a free foundation assessment. Ignoring these warning signs can lead to exponentially greater structural damage and significantly higher repair costs down the road.

Our foundation inspectors will visit your home, document all findings with photos and measurements, and provide you with an honest, detailed written report — along with a full range of repair options and transparent pricing. There is never any pressure or obligation.

  • Cracks in walls, floors, or ceilings
  • Doors or windows that stick or won't close
  • Uneven or sloping floors
  • Water seeping through basement walls
  • Bowing or leaning foundation walls
  • Musty odors or visible mold growth
